# Retail-Data
The amount spent by a customer is a central indicator to know a retail company's health. In order to keep it's best customers and attract new ones, the company must apply specific strategies, otherwise customers may not purchase anymore.

The RFM criteria allows to segment customers into different categories, according to their last purchase date, number of purchases and total amount spent. It's a good method to know which customers are the bests, which need some efforts and which are endangered or close to be lost. Depending on the segment the customer belongs to, different marketing offers can be applied to increase yields.

As the report shows, the majority of customers have the same profile, buy the same articles and in the same way. However, charateristics specific to each segment also appear and it's possible to apply temporary offers, return offers, product-based offers or free shippings for each segment. These strategies should increase yields.
